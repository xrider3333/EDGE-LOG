▸Mutual Information (MI)Stage 3 / 5PLANNED
What it isScores any dependency between a param/feature and PnL — linear or not. Why it mattersPearson misses inverted-U / threshold relationships (good in a mid-range, bad at extremes → r ≈ 0). MI catches them. Hooks / how it's computedsklearn.feature_selection.mutual_info_regression, which uses the KSG (Kraskov–Stögbauer–Grassberger, 2004) k-nearest-neighbor estimator under the hood — that's the math inside MI, not a separate score.
▸Predictive Power Score (PPS)Stage 3 / 5PLANNED
What it isA single intuitive 0–1 score of how well X predicts Y — asymmetric (X→Y ≠ Y→X), nonlinear, handles categoricals. Why it mattersOne readable number to rank params/features by real predictive power; better than r for screening. Hooks / caveatppscore package. Default model is a decision tree → use cross-validated PPS so it doesn't overfit tiny samples.

▸Adversarial-validation gateStage 5PLANNED
What it isTrain a classifier to tell fit-era bars from recent / lockbox bars. If it can, the regime shifted. Why it mattersCatches a champion fit to a stale world. Complements transfer + lockbox. Hooks intovalidate.py — one more entry in the checks dict.
▸Conformal band on the gatesStage 5PLANNED
What it isWrap the fold / lockbox estimate in a calibrated interval; gate on the lower bound. Why it mattersPoint thresholds (PnL>0, PF≥1) ignore small-sample noise; "lower bound > 0" is harder to fool. Hooks / toolsvalidate.py gate logic; MAPIE / Venn-ABERS.
▸Causal counterfactual checkStage 5PLANNED
What it is"What's the PnL on these same bars without the signal?" — the counterfactual. Why it mattersSeparates a real entry edge from just being long in a rising market. Sharper than the SPY benchmark. Hooks / toolsvalidate.py; CausalImpact / interrupted-time-series.
